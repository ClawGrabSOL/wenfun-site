2. The Loop
  • Claim — Creator fees claimed from pump.fun every cycle.
  • Budget — Fees + vault combined into swap budget.
  • Swap — Full budget routed into tokenized $WEN via Jupiter.
  • Snapshot — All $WENFUN holders fetched. Wallets below $10 filtered out.
  • Distribute — $WEN tokens sent to every eligible holder.
  • Repeat — Every 5 minutes.
